      What are the complications of WAGR syndrome?
      It is important to know that an individual with WAGR syndrome may have some of these conditions but will not have all of them.
      • Auditory and Sensory Processing Disorders.
      • BDNF Gene Deletion.
      • Behavioral or psychiatric disorders.
      • Cardiopulmonary abnormalities.
      • Chronic kidney disease.
      • Head, Ears, Nose, and Throat abnormalities.
